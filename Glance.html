    Beacon at a glance

    Beacon Pharmaceuticals PLC is a renowned pharmaceutical company in Bangladesh that comes to light for its innovative treatment approaches for the betterment of the quality of life. Beacon was established as a company in 2006 and became a Public Limited Company in 2010. Through continuous research and development, the company ensures providing innovative pharmaceutical solutions to the people of Bangladesh and the world at large, thus ensuring stakeholders' satisfaction.

    The pharmaceutical company manufactures anti-cancer, cardiovascular, gastrointestinal, antibiotics, anti-coagulants, protein supplements, muscle relaxants, antihistamines, analgesics, NSAIDS, and more. Hence, it ensures the image of launching unique products and the fastest-growing medicine industry in Bangladesh.

    The state-of-the-art manufacturing Plant is located at Bhaluka, Mymensingh, Bangladesh covers 30 acres of land area. The European experts who designed this Beacon industrial facility made it one of the best in the nation. It is made to meet international standards set by organizations like the WHO-CGMP, US FDA, UK MHRA, TGA, and Australia.

    It is also a matter of great pride for BEACON that the company has achieved the prestigious EU GMP certificate for its dedicated cephalosporin facility.
